16-based RISC-based Cryptographic Processor

With growing security concerns in this digital age, tamper-resistant hardware is a necessity, in case of, self-driving cars and electronic voting machines. For addressing such concerns, this project aims at designing and implementing a 16-bit application-specific instruction-set processor which aims at efficiently (in terms of clock-cyles) performing cryptographic operations.

The project also involves the study of power consumption and area requirements by the above processor.
